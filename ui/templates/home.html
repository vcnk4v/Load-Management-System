PROJECT DESCRIPTION

            
            
            
            
    Our objective is to maximize the utilization 
    of solar energy and minimize the use of 
    conventional energy while ensuring a stable 
    power supply to meet the load demand. 
    The model is designed to meet the load 
    demand at all times with the best possible source. 
    Moreover it will be scalable and adaptable
    to different situational requirements 
    and solar energy production levels.
